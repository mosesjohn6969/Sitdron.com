Find Out More About Sitdron Enterprises

Sitdron Enterprise is a company established in 2013 by a group of friends who thought it wise that the world was burdened with a whole load of care. They decided to establish a company formerly known as Brainz Intelligence which later became Brelite before assuming its permanent name as Sitdron Enterprise. The aim of its establishment was to bring ease and to meet the daily demands of the world through technological ideas. It was a company set – up to bring innovation and invention to the common man. Its idea rest with alleviating the burden of people in various sector of life.

  • Mission

    - To bring enlightenment to the world through technology.
    - To inspire moments of optimism and happiness.
    - To create value and make a difference.
    - Great place to work & inspired people to be the best they can be.

  • Vision

    To use technology as a tool for the betterment of life
